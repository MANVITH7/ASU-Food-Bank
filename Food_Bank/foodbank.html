What is ASU Food Bank about?

The ASU Food Bank, which is open to all Arizona State University students with a valid student ID, is a valuable resource dedicated to addressing food insecurity among college students. Fresh fruits and vegetables, various breads, proteins, canned goods, and nonperishable items are among the essentials we offer. Our initiative aims to provide free access to nutritious food options while also creating a supportive environment for students facing food security challenges.
